Details

Afang-soup

  • Chop and pound or gring the afang leaves
  • Chop the waterleaf
  • Blend the pepper and crayfish
  • Prep and cook the meat/stock and perewinkle

Cook the stock till the water is almost dried up, pour in the waterleaf and steam for 5mins in minimal heat.
add the pepper, crayfish, perewinkle, seasoning, mix to taste. allow to steam for 1min, add 2cooking spoon of palmoil
add the grinded afang and stir, allow to cook for 5mins, food is ready. consume with swallow of your choice.

Stew

  • Wash and blend the fresh tomatoes
  • Blend peper and onions
  • chop small qty of onions aside
  • Boil 3-4 fresh eggs
  • Prep and steam the meat
  • prep stew spices of your choice

Place a dry pot on the stove, add 2 cooking spoon of groundnut.oil, heat for 1min, pour in chopped onions, add tomatoes pastes
and stir for 10mins till its properly cooked, add blended onions and pepper into pot and mix. Add seasonings, meat, boiled eggs
and spices, mix to taste. Add blended tomatoes into pot, stir and allow to cook for 10-15mins.Food is ready.
